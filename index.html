Who We Are

Fruit of Education is a non-profit organisation dedicated to supporting learners in accessing and fully benefiting from education. We believe that education is a powerful pathway to discovering and fulfilling one’s God-given purpose. Recognising the various internal and external challenges that can hinder the successful completion of an educational journey, we are committed to fostering an environment that values, supports, and promotes educational growth.


Our Mission

To provide a clear, safe, and God-guided pathway that enables South African youth and learners to fulfil their purpose through education.


The Vision

To develop successful young people into industry experts who lead purposeful and thriving careers.

Programs

📚 Mentorship

Each learner is provided with the opportunity to receive mentorship in their chosen career field from an experienced professional. Mentorship sessions are conducted on a weekly basis.

📚 Tutorials

We provide targeted academic tutorials to support learners in strengthening and improving their academic performance.

📚 Mass Applications

We support learners in applying to higher education institutions and bursaries by offering guidance on motivational letters, personalised feedback on applications, and effective application strategies.

📚 Self-Development Drive

We host a business mentorship programme, Do Business Til I Come, which facilitates regular workshops aimed at developing entrepreneurial skills and supporting the creation of successful, sustainable businesses. In addition, we conduct regular employment workshops to support job seekers with interview preparation, effective job-hunting strategies, and CV writing, editing, and review.

📚 Career Exploration Events

We host monthly events that empower learners to discover their purpose by exploring their identity, building their competencies, and identifying their passions.
